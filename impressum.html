Services

StoreNGo provides secure luggage storage services for travelers in Interlaken, Switzerland. Our services include:

  • Short-term and long-term luggage storage
  • Secure storage for bags, suitcases, and backpacks
  • Convenient location near Interlaken Ost train station
  • Customer support via WhatsApp and phone

Liability

aversis GmbH operates StoreNGo luggage storage service. While we take every precaution to ensure the security of stored items, luggage is stored at the customer's own risk. We recommend that customers:

  • Keep valuable items, passports, and important documents with them
  • Ensure proper insurance coverage for their belongings
  • Follow our terms and conditions for storage
